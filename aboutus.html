WHAT CHA TALKING ABOUT

The word ‘cha’ means tea. However Chatime means much more than just ‘teatime’. We’re changing the way the world drinks tea.

In 2005, Henry founded the very first Chatime in Taiwan, with a commitment to quality ingredients and mix-ins and a flare for innovative flavour combinations, he set out to brew nothing but the very best iced teas.

At Chatime you can customise your sweetness and ice level to your liking. We offer a range of milk teas, fresh teas, tea espresso, fruit teas, tea lattes, smoothies, juices, and coffee! We use only real tea leaves and brew all our tea fresh in store. Every cup is made to order by our professionally trained tearista. So what are you waiting for? Come grab a cuppa tea and refresh your thirst!
